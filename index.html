Company Profile

ABD Techno Pvt Ltd manufactures windmill spare parts, hydraulic parts, TAFE parts, and automobile components. We focus on precision machining, quality control, and on-time delivery.

Business

  • Windmill spare parts (hubs, shafts, brake components, wear parts)
  • Hydraulic components (cylinders, valves, manifolds, fittings)
  • TAFE / agri-machinery parts (OEM-compatible spares)
  • Automobile components (machined & sub-assemblies)

Quality systems, material traceability, and preventive maintenance support consistent output.
